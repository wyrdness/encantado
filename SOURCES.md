### When Citing Encantado Stories

Always acknowledge:
```
Traditional Amazonian folklore, synthesized from indigenous 
Amazonian and Portuguese cultural elements, actively maintained 
by Amazon river communities.
```

### Cultural Acknowledgment

The Encantado belongs to Amazonian cultural heritage and serves living social and conservation functions. This folklore:
- Provides social explanations for complex situations
- Protects endangered river dolphins through cultural reverence
- Represents synthesis of indigenous and colonial cultures
- Is actively believed and practiced in Amazon communities
